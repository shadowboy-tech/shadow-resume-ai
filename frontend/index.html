About

Physics-Informed MRI Reconstruction for Low-Field MRI

Low-field MRI scanners produce noisy, low-resolution images with long scan times — a real barrier in resource-limited settings like Nigeria. I extended the End-to-End Variational Network with physics-informed priors from the MRI forward model, built a full k-space preprocessing pipeline with retrospective undersampling, and validated reconstruction quality across multiple acceleration factors using SSIM, PSNR, and NMSE.
